About Me

I work as a Postdoctoral Associate at Robarts Research Institute, Schulich School of Medicine & Dentistry, ​​Western University, Canada. Before that, I worked as a medical physicist at Dong Nai General Hospital from 2009 to 2020 before becoming a lecturer in the Department of Industrial Electronics-Biomedical Engineering at the Faculty of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at HCMC University of Technology and Education (HCMUTE) from 2020 to 2022.

I do research in Biomedical Engineering and Medical Physics. My research interest is on Monte Carlo Simulations, Radiation therapy, Medical imaging process, Deep learning in medical imaging, and radiation therapy.

My current project is working on Halcyon LINAC and Theranostics
